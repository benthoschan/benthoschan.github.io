My own path towards research in ocean science was not linear and, were it not for small events that lined up and instructors that were passionate about their research, I would not have pursued this path. My approach to teaching focuses on meeting students where they're at while pushing them to challenge themselves. My MO is to facilitate hands on experiences and finding ways to relate their learning to real-life examples that are accessible to everyone in the class. Additionally, it's essential that I emphasize that ocean science is a system science -- that everything is interconnected regardless of if you live inland or close to the ocean and that there is still so much to explore on Earth.
